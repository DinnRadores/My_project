Adobong sitaw

Ingredients:

  • 250g string beans (sitaw)
  • 150g pork belly, sliced
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 3 tablespoons soy sauce
  • 2 tablespoons vinegar
  • 1 cup water
  • 1 bay leaf
  • 1 teaspoon whole peppercorns
  • 2 tablespoons cooking oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Procedure:

  1. Heat oil in a pan over medium heat.
  2. Saut√© garlic and onions until fragrant.
  3. Add pork belly slices and cook until browned.
  4. Pour soy sauce, vinegar, and water into the pan. Add bay leaf and peppercorns. Let it simmer for 15 minutes.
  5. Add string beans (sitaw) and cook until tender, about 5-7 minutes.
  6. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  7. Serve hot with steamed rice.
