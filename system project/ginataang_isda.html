Ginataang tilapia

ingredients

  • Tilapia fillets
  • Coconut milk (gata)
  • Garlic, minced
  • Onion, chopped
  • Ginger, sliced
  • Chili peppers (optional)
  • Fish sauce (patis)
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • cassava leaves (optional)

    • Procedure:

    1. Clean the tilapia fillets thoroughly under running water, then pat them dry with paper towels.
    2. In a large skillet or pot, heat some oil over medium heat. Saut√© minced garlic, chopped onion, and sliced ginger until fragrant.
    3. If desired, add chili peppers for some heat.
    4. Pour in the coconut milk (gata) and bring it to a gentle simmer.
    5. Add fish sauce (patis) to taste, and season with salt and pepper.
    6. Add the tilapia fillets to the simmering coconut milk mixture.
    7. Cover the skillet or pot and let the tilapia cook for about 8-10 minutes or until fully cooked through.
    8. If desired, add chopped vegetables such as spinach, bok choy, or string beans during the last few minutes of cooking.
    9. Once the tilapia is cooked and the vegetables are tender, remove the skillet or pot from the heat.
    10. Serve the Ginataang Tilapia hot with steamed rice.
