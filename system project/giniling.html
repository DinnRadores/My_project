giniling

Ingredients:

  • 500g ground pork
  • 2 tablespoons cooking oil
  • 1 medium onion, chopped
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 medium potato, diced
  • 1 medium carrot, diced
  • 1 red bell pepper, diced
  • 1 green bell pepper, diced
  • 1 cup green peas
  • 1 cup tomato sauce
  • 1 cup water
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Procedure:

  1. Heat cooking oil in a pan over medium heat.
  2. Saute onions and garlic until fragrant.
  3. Add ground pork and cook until it turns brown.
  4. Add potatoes, carrots, and bell peppers. Cook for 5 minutes.
  5. Pour in tomato sauce and water. Stir well.
  6. Bring to a boil then simmer for 15-20 minutes or until the vegetables are tender.
  7. Add green peas and season with salt and pepper. Cook for another 5 minutes.
  8. Serve hot with steamed rice. Enjoy!
