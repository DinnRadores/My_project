Kare-kare

ingredients

  • 2 lbs ox tail, cut into 2-inch lengths
  • 1 lb beef tripe, cleaned and cut into bite-sized pieces
  • 1 cup peanut butter
  • 2 eggplants, sliced diagonally
  • 1 bundle string beans (sitaw), cut into 2-inch lengths
  • 1 bundle bok choy (pechay), separated into stalks
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 4 tablespoons annatto seeds (achuete)
  • 4 cups beef broth
  • 2 tablespoons cooking oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste

    • Procedure:

    1. In a large pot, boil ox tail and beef tripe in water until tender. Drain and set aside.
    2. In a separate pan, heat cooking oil and sauté garlic and onions until fragrant.
    3. Add annatto seeds and cook until the oil turns red. Remove the seeds and discard.
    4. Add the boiled ox tail and beef tripe. Sauté for a few minutes.
    5. Pour in the beef broth and bring to a boil. Simmer for about an hour or until the meat is tender.
    6. Add peanut butter and stir until well combined. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
    7. Add the eggplants, string beans, and bok choy. Cook until the vegetables are tender.
    8. Adjust seasoning if necessary. Serve hot with bagoong (shrimp paste) on the side.
