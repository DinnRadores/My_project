Munggo

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup dried monggo (mung beans)
  • 4 cups water
  • 2 tablespoons cooking oil
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 tomatoes, chopped
  • 1/2 lb pork belly or pork shoulder, diced (optional)
  • 1 Knorr pork or shrimp cube (optional)
  • 1 bunch spinach or malunggay leaves, cleaned and plucked
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Procedure:

  1. Wash the monggo beans thoroughly, then soak them in water for at least 1 hour or overnight. Drain before cooking.
  2. In a pot, heat cooking oil over medium heat. Saut√© garlic, onions, and tomatoes until fragrant.
  3. If using, add diced pork and cook until lightly browned.
  4. Add the soaked monggo beans to the pot. Pour in water and bring to a boil.
  5. If using, add Knorr cube for added flavor. Stir to dissolve.
  6. Reduce heat to low and simmer until monggo beans are soft and starting to break down, about 45 minutes to 1 hour, stirring occasionally.
  7. Once the beans are soft and the stew has thickened, add spinach or malunggay leaves. Cook for another 2-3 minutes until the leaves are wilted.
  8. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  9. Serve hot with steamed rice.
