Beef pares

Ingredients:

  • 1 kg beef brisket, cubed
  • 3 tablespoons cooking oil
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 6 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 thumb-sized ginger, sliced
  • 1/2 cup soy sauce
  • 1/4 cup brown sugar
  • 1 beef bouillon cube
  • 6 cups water
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Spring onions, chopped (for garnish)
  • Chili flakes (optional, for added spice)

Procedure:

  1. Heat cooking oil in a pot over medium heat.
  2. Saute chopped onions until translucent.
  3. Add minced garlic and saute until fragrant.
  4. Add the cubed beef brisket and cook until browned on all sides.
  5. Pour water into the pot, enough to cover the beef.
  6. Add soy sauce, brown sugar, and beef bouillon cube. Stir to combine.
  7. Bring the mixture to a boil, then reduce heat to low and let it simmer for 1.5 to 2 hours or until the beef is tender.
  8. Once the beef is tender, remove it from the pot and set aside.
  9. Strain the remaining liquid to remove any impurities.
  10. Return the strained liquid to the pot and bring to a boil.
  11. In a separate pan, heat cooking oil and fry the beef until browned and slightly crispy.
  12. Add the fried beef back to the pot with the boiling liquid.
  13. Simmer for an additional 10-15 minutes until the sauce thickens.
  14. Adjust seasoning with salt and pepper if needed.
  15. Serve hot with steamed rice and enjoy!
