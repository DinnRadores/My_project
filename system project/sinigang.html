pork sinigang

Ingredients:

  • 1 lb (450g) pork ribs or pork belly, cut into serving pieces
  • 1 onion, quartered
  • 2 tomatoes, quartered
  • 1 radish (labanos), sliced
  • 1 eggplant (talong), sliced
  • 10-12 pieces string beans (sitaw), cut into 2-inch pieces
  • 2 pieces green chili peppers (siling haba)
  • 1 packet (40g) sinigang mix (tamarind soup base)
  • 8 cups water
  • Salt to taste

Procedure:

  1. In a large pot, bring water to a boil.
  2. Add pork ribs or pork belly and let it simmer until meat is tender.
  3. Add onions, tomatoes, and radish. Let it simmer for 5 minutes.
  4. Add eggplant, string beans, and green chili peppers. Simmer for another 5 minutes.
  5. Add sinigang mix and stir well. Simmer for 3-5 minutes.
  6. Season with salt to taste.
  7. Remove from heat and serve hot with steamed rice.
