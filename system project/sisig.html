pork belly sisig

Ingredients:

  • Pork belly or pork jowl, chopped
  • Chicken liver, chopped
  • Onions, minced
  • Garlic, minced
  • Green chili peppers, minced
  • Red chili peppers, minced
  • Calamansi or lemon juice
  • Soy sauce
  • Salt
  • Black pepper
  • Mayonnaise
  • Oil for frying

Procedure:

  1. Boil the pork belly or jowl until tender. Drain and let it cool, then chop into small pieces.
  2. In a separate pan, fry the chopped chicken liver until cooked. Remove from the pan and chop into small pieces.
  3. In the same pan, saut√© minced onions and garlic until translucent.
  4. Add the chopped pork and liver to the pan. Cook until slightly browned and crispy.
  5. Add minced green and red chili peppers. Stir well.
  6. Season with soy sauce, salt, and black pepper to taste.
  7. Squeeze calamansi or lemon juice over the mixture.
  8. Optionally, mix in mayonnaise for creaminess.
  9. Serve hot and enjoy!
